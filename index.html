About me

I am taking this course to gain the knowledge and exprience to make and manage my own website for my Art career. I came from construction, my background in construction was in the natural gas industry. I was a first responder to natural gas leaks and hit lines. The emergency response required alot of focus and skills around public safety. I started my art with carving wood by hand and progressed into using epoxy with my wood carvings to add another aspect to my carvings.
